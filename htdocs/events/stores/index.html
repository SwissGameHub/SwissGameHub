Game Stores Workshop

  • Friday, 26.6.2020, 19:00
  • Swiss Game Hub, Hohlstrasse 176, 8004 Zürich
  • No entry fee, no RSVP required, open to everyone

What places are there to sell your game? How do you use them effectively? A deep dive into the Steam backend, store pages, tagging, and more.

Stray Fawn, Blindflug Studios, and David Stark have long and up to date experience selling PC games on Steam, GOG, Humble, itch, mobile, consoles, and other platforms. We will share and discuss our experiences using these platforms, best practices for presenting your game, and our traffic and sales numbers.

If you want to figure out which platforms to sell your game on, or you're already selling games and want to swap knowledge, this is the event for you.

This is the second of a series of events run by Swiss Game Hub aimed at professional game developers and those who aspire to be. Even better, it's not on Valentine's day this time!
